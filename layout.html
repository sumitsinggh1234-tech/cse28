Science is built on doubt: it is a culture where everything, including supposed “truths,” can and should be questioned

If you want to understand nature, you must focus on how things actually work, not just on fancy words or definitions

Knowing the label for something is very different from truly understanding it; real knowledge comes from seeing how it behaves and why

The most important rule is: do not deceive yourself, because you are the person you can fool most easily.

Scientific integrity means constantly trying to prove yourself wrong, not just looking for evidence that you are right

Sumit Singh

CSE-28

It is more exciting to live with doubt and uncertainty than to cling to answers that might be wrong
